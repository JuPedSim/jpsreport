Requirements

  1. CMake
  2. A compiler with support for C++11 e.g. g++ or clang or Visual Studio 2013
  3. for JPSvis and JPSeditor additionally Qt and VTK are needed.
  4. JPSreport and JPScore need Boost-libraries. At the moment boost is hard-linked in JPSreport, but this should be fixed in the future.
